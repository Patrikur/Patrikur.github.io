Emergency Services

I do not offer emergency services. Should you need help NOW in this moment these are places you can contact. Please do not hesitate to reach out to them – they are there for you, now this minute.


Red Cross Helpline

The Helpline´s main role is to provide active listening and advice on community resources for people of all ages in need. Annually they receive about 15 thousands contacts that are as diverse as they are many. The volunteers are extensively trained, experienced and of all ages.

The helpline is also available via online chat. When connecting to the chat, they ask for a name and an e-mail address. Keep in mind that you do not have to provide your right name or e-mail. Complete confidentiality and anonymity is promised. If you do not receive an answer soon after you have been connected, it´s because their chat is busy at the moment and they ask you to wait for the next available counselor. If you cannot wait you can leave a message and they will get back to you as soon as possible within the next 24-hours.

Landspitali Hospital

The Emergency Psychiatric Department is located on the ground floor of the Psychiatric building at Landspitali Hringbraut. It is for people experiencing acute psychiatric conditions and it is not necessary to book an appointment. You can also call and get a consultation/advise from a Nurse during the opening hours.

Opening hours: Monday - Friday 12:00 – 19:00; Weekend and holidays 13:00 - 17:00.

Phone: +3545434050 (only during opening hours)

When the Psychiatric Emergency Department is closed you can go to the General Emergency Department at Landspitali Fossvogur.
